 Dhaka Incubator & Electronic - Master Project Prompt
Project Goal: Create a high-end, high-conversion e-commerce landing page for "Dhaka Incubator & Electronic," specializing in egg incubators and electronics in Bangladesh.
Core Technology: React 18+, Tailwind CSS (Modern Engine), Framer Motion (for animations), and Lucide-React (for icons).
1. Design & Aesthetic:
Style: "Clean Utility" mixed with "Modern SaaS." High contrast, professional, and trustworthy.
Color Palette: Primary Brand Orange (#ff6a00), Neutral Grays, and Pure White.
Typography: Dual-language support. Use "Inter" for English and "Hind Siliguri" for professional Bengali rendering.
Navigation: A "Glass-morphism" fixed navbar with a language toggle (EN/BN) and a pulse-animated shopping bag icon.
2. Hero Section:
Background: A full-screen, looped cinematic video (grayscale/darkened) showing mechanical or industrial movement.
Content: Bold, oversized white typography with a "Premier Collection" badge.
CTAs: A primary "Shop Products" button (scrolls to products) and a secondary "Contact Sales" button (triggers a tel: dialer).
3. Interactive Product Grid:
Layout: 2x2 grid on mobile, 3-column grid on desktop.
Filtering: Functional filter bar for categories: "All", "Incubator", "Fan", and "Gadgets".
Product Cards: Hover-effect cards showing a "Limited Stock" badge, price, and a quick "Add to Cart" (+) button.
Product Details Modal: Clicking a card opens a large modal with a split layout: Big high-res image on the left, detailed description and technical specifications list on the right.
4. Trust & Info System:
Badges: Four interactive badges (COD, 7-Day Replacement, 24/7 Support, Nationwide Delivery).
Popups: Clicking badges opens dedicated informational modals. The "Nationwide Delivery" badge must open an embedded Google Map of Bangladesh.
5. Shopping Cart & Checkout:
Drawer: A modern slide-out cart drawer from the right.
Functionality: Quantity adjustment (+/-), item removal, and a persistent grand total.
Checkout Form: A shortened, high-UX form (Name, Phone, Address).
Order Fulfillment: Clicking "Confirm Order" generates a pre-filled, formatted WhatsApp message for the business owner including the customer details and an itemized receipt.
6. Footer & Contact:
Quick Links: "Home" (smooth scroll to top), "Products" (scroll to grid), and "Warranty" (opens info popup).
Social/Action Icons:
Phone icon (triggers tel: dialer).
Message icon (triggers sms: messaging).
Location icon (links to Google Maps search for the office).
